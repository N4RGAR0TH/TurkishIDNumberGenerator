INFO

The algorithm used to create Turkish ID numbers is the following:
  1. Randomly generate the first nine digits.
  2. Get the numbers from odd digits (1, 3, 5, 7 and 9th digits) and combine them.
  3. Multiply the result with 7.
  4. Get the numbers from even digits (2, 4, 6 and 8th digits) and combine them.
  5. Subtract the combined even digit variable from the combined odd digit variable and modulate it with 10.
  6. Add that number to the 10th digit.
  7. Get every ten digit of the ID number and combine them and after that, modulate it with 10.
  8. Add that number to the 11th digit.
